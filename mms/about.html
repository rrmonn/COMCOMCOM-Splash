MUSIC PRODUCTION & AUDIOVISUAL EXPERIENCES
Exploring artistic expressions to find transformational value.

Mad Monk Studios® explores the heights of transcendence through music production and audiovisual experiences. Based in Mexico City, MMS creates a unique artistic platform that offers the infrastructure and expertise to help artists discover, transform and showcase their exceptional talents in a universal way.

HARNESSING THE POWER OF SPACE AND SOUND TO EVOKE STATES OF AWE.

At the crossroads of economic, political & social changes we believe our world needs moments of reflection, harmony and awe. Committed to the social impact of art, Mad Monk Studios® unveils an alternative process of artistic creation, providing a temple for artists to synchronize the energies of stillness and action, find their ‘why’ and transform it into honest, globally competitive productions to offer their audiences mindbending experiences with cultural relevance, positive impact and an opportunity to connect within.
